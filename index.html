Research Interests

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Systems Engineering at RME, part of the Distributed Computing Research Group (DisCo) of the Universidad de Zaragoza (Spain) focusing on software and system security; where I am supervised by Prof. Ricardo J. Rodríguez, and funded by the Gobierno de Aragón. My research interests include malware analysis, reverse engineering, network security, and digital forensics.
