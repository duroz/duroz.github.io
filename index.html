Daniel Uroz Hinarejos

I am a first year PhD Student in Cyber Security at the Distributed Computing Group (DisCo) of the University of Zaragoza (Spain), where I am under the supervision of Prof. Javier Campos and Prof. Ricardo J. Rodríguez, and funded by the Government of Aragón.

Previously, I have worked as a Research Assistant at University of Zaragoza, mainly investigating new malware detection techniques in computer forensics. I have also worked as a Malware Analyst at S21Sec, reversing engineering malware and developing tools to detect and automatically extract information of malware samples.

I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at University of Zaragoza, and I am finishing a master's degree in Research in Cyber Security at University of León (Spain).
